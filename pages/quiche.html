Eddy's Crustless Quiche

This is a simple meal prep receipe I have been making for years now to have a healthy breakfast or lunch at work

Ingredients:

  • Eggs x 16 Large
  • Ground Turkey x 1 lbs
  • Kale x 1 bag
  • Potatoes

Steps:

  1. Cook ground beef
  2. Mix veggies with meat
  3. Mix eggs into meat and veggies
  4. Bake everything @ 420 degrees for 30mins
  5. Cut and enjoy it
