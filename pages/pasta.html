Eddy's Pesto n' Eggs

My favorite pasta receipe

Ingredients:

  • Eggs x 2 large
  • Peppered Bacon x 1 Slice
  • Pesto Sauce x 1 Jar
  • Pasta x 1 box

Steps:

  1. CIn a pot, boil water and cook pasta. In a pan, crisp the bacon
  2. Once the pasta is cooked, throw in the bacon slices and crack the eggs into the pan to cook eggs with heat from pasta
  3. Serve and enjoy.
