Eddy's Breakfast Tacos

A fun little breakfast taco receipe

Ingredients (for 1 taco):

  • Eggs x 1 Large/li>
  • Peppered Bacon x 1 Slice
  • Kale x 1/4 cup
  • Corn Tortilla x 1

Steps:

  1. Crisp the torilla up a bit on the pan
  2. Crips up the bacon on the pan.
  3. Throw the kale in the pan to wilt it.
  4. Cook the eggs in the mixture.
  5. Serve and enjoy it
